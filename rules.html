This is not a PG community if strong language and/or dark humor offends you, this community is not for you.

Rules

1) You must be 18+ to join Ancient Relic. If you are caught lying about your age, you will be banned instantly


2) Do not grief, troll or cause deliberate damage to teammates in any game, under any circumstances.


3a) Absolutely no cheating in competitive PvP multiplayer games. Ex; No use of 3rd party software for the intent of gaining an advantage over other players, No exploiting; no breaking/clipping out of a map, or using a glitch to gain an advantage, etc.


3b) Exploits will be tolerated in PVE content to an extent. If you are asked to stop, you must comply, or you will be kicked from the game/match and potentially receive a temporary/ permanent ban.


4) VAC bans on steam will result in an instant ban.



Code of Conduct

1) Don't be a dick.

- Do not cause unnecessary drama with your conduct. If you insult someone, apologize, make up and move on.
-Personal drama is to be kept off voice channels, take it elsewhere.
-Do not discuss politics, religion, or ethics in voice comms or chat. All debates on this subject can be taken to private discord calls/chats.
-If you have a dispute or complaint with another member, contact a Moderator or an Officer.
-DO NOT rudely interupt the server master or an Officer while they are talking with a member of the community or a new member in the Private voice channel.
-DO NOT argue with a moderator about how they do their job, if it continues a temp ban will be given. If you have an issue with a moderator, bring it up with an Officer or the Server Master.

2) Do not spam chat ie; no single words per line in sequence, no spamming the same words over and over, etc.


3) Do not use game specific chat rooms if you are not playing that game. If you must contact a member in that voice chat room, either message them or enter the voice chat briefly to get their attention, then move to a different chat room.


4) Have fun! We are a just for fun gaming community, we are not bound by any specific game, feel free to play what you like. However, we also will have semi organized events for games that a large amount of people play, and you are more than welcome to join in.



Notes

-Have a favorite multiplayer game? Bring it up in general chat, most are looking for something new or different to try out.

-Are you a Youtuber or Streamer? Contact Qrow(Confuzuru) via Discord or The Forum and he will add a link to your channel on the community website.


Violations

Violation of the Rules or Code of Conduct under any circumstances is punishable by bans under a 3 strike system. First strike will result in a warning, Second strike will resault in a 24-48 hour ban, Third strike you will be banned for a week. After any more strikes, you will be banned permanently. If you feel that you have been banned unfairly, you may appeal your case to the Server Master/Officers by using the Ban Appeal Section in the forums. You may also use The Halls of The Banned Discord chat room, but we ask you use the forum to make it easier to keep track of the appeal process.
